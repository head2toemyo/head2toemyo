What is myofunctional therapy?

A therapy approach that supports healthy oral and facial function.

Myofunctional therapy is the neuromuscular re-education of the oral and facial muscles. Through this therapy you are carving new neural pathways between the brain and the muscles of the mouth, tongue, and face. It can help improve breathing patterns, tongue posture, swallowing, and oral habits that can affect sleep, comfort, speech support, and overall wellness.
